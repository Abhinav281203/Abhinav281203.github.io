Perplexity-imitate

A Python application designed to replicate the functionality of Perplexity's search engine. Upon receiving a search query, top articles from internet are picked and most relevant ones are sent to generate a comprehensive summary. Using YAKE algorithm, the application extracts keywords from the summary, helping in creating a new context that is then utilized for further searches, giving suggested articles to the user's query.

The application offers multilingual support in languages such as English, Telugu, Kannada, and Hindi.
